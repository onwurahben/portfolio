Meeting Analysis Bot

Role

Cloud AI Engineer

Focus

Enterprise Automation

Stack

AWS Lambda, GPT-4, S3

The Challenge

Following up on long meetings is a major productivity drain. Summary emails often miss critical action items and speaker context.

The Solution

This production-grade analysis bot processes meeting transcripts to identify speaker sentiment, key decisions, and automated task assignments for enterprise teams.

Scalability

Serverless architecture handling 1000+ hours monthly

Special Feature

Automated Jira integration for action item tracking.
