Redraft Content System

Role

Lead AI Engineer

Focus

Generative Pipelines

Stack

Claude 3, Next.js, PostgreSQL

The Challenge

Creating high-quality social content at scale often leads to a drop in brand voice consistency and engagement.

The Solution

Redraft is an end-to-end content system that uses LLMs to generate, evaluate, and approve LinkedIn content based on specific persona guidelines and engagement metrics.

Efficiency

90% faster content drafting

Compliance

Built-in AI "Judge" model to ensure every post adheres to brand safety and style rules.
